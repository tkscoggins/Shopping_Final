Recycling Dreams

Whenever I make a purchase, it has an intended purpose. I buy things with a vision in mind for how I will use it, how it will make me feel, how it will help me acheive a goal. Sometimes, that purchase does everything I hoped for and more. Other times, it is discarded or forgotten. That same item can be used to fulfill someone else's dream. Maybe a dress I bought to help me get a job, you'd see as part of an ensemble to make you the life of the party. Here are the things that comprised my old dreams. Look.
